01

Install Nooriva

Tap Download app. If your browser does not show the prompt, use its install or add-to-home-screen option.

02

Enable notifications

Open the Prayer page and allow notifications when asked. Installed Nooriva works best when opened from your home screen.

03

Prayer checkboxes

Mark prayers as completed from the Prayer page. They reset automatically each day at midnight in Malawi time.

04

Tasbeeh counter

Use Tasbeeh for quick dhikr counting with saved progress on this device.

05

Offline basics

Nooriva now keeps key screens available even if your connection is weak or temporarily lost.

Need support?

Use the contact section on Home for updates, questions, or early collaboration requests.
